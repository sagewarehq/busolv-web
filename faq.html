Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Busolv

How does Busolv track my calories? +
Busolv allows you to log your meals by either text input or taking a picture of your meal. We use AI technology to estimate calories and macronutrients to provide you with the most accurate data.
Is the photo scan feature accurate? +
We use advanced image recognition technology to accurately estimate the calories and macronutrients in your meal. The technology is constantly improving, so you can expect even better results over time.
Can I customize my calorie and macro goals? +
Yes, you can customize your calorie and macro goals. Go to Home, and then Profile at the top right corner of the screen. Click on edit to edit your profile and goals.
How do I update my weight? +
To update your weight go to Home, and then Profile at the top right corner of the screen. Click on edit to edit your profile and weight. You can also go to Home, and then to Summary, scroll down and click on Update Weight.
Is there a free trial? +
Yes, we offer a free 7-day trial. Sign up for a free trial today to see Busolv for yourself!
How do I delete my account and all my data? +
Go to the Settings tab in the Busolv app. Under Manage Account, open Account Management. Tap Clear Data to remove all stored data, or tap Delete Account to permanently delete your account.
